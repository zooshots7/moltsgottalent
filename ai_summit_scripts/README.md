## 🔧 TECHNICAL SPECS SUMMARY

### Video Export Standards

**Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts:**
- Resolution: 1080x1920 (9:16)
- Frame rate: 30fps (IG) / 60fps (YT)
- Codec: H.264
- Max size: 4GB (IG) / unlimited (YT)

**Twitter/X:**
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (16:9) or 1080x1080 (1:1)
- Frame rate: 30fps
- Codec: H.264
- Max size: 512MB
- Max duration: 2:20 (most users), 10 min (some accounts)

**LinkedIn:**
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (16:9) or 1080x1080 (1:1)
- Frame rate: 30fps
- Codec: H.264
- Max size: 5GB
- Max duration: 10 min

**YouTube (Main):**
- Resolution: 3840x2160 (4K preferred) or 1920x1080 (HD)
- Frame rate: 30fps or 60fps
- Codec: H.264 or H.265
- Max size: 256GB
- Max duration: 12 hours (but keep it concise)

### Audio

- Sample rate: 48kHz
- Bit depth: 16-bit minimum, 24-bit preferred
- Channels: Stereo
- Loudness: -14 LUFS (broadcast standard)

### Captions

- Format: SRT or VTT
- Languages: English (primary), Hindi (secondary)
- Burnt-in for social (always)
- Separate file for YouTube (accessibility)
